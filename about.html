About Me

Hi, I’m Kelvin Wanyama. I studied Computer Security and Forensics at Kabarak University and specialize in cybersecurity analysis, web development, and IT support.

I’m passionate about safeguarding the digital space, building secure systems, and leveraging technology to solve real-world problems. I’ve co-founded Roofa.co.ke, a housing platform expanding across Kenya, and I also serve as Managing Director at SFH Solution Ltd in Diani Beach.

I’ve participated in hackathons and security challenges — from building tools like a Baseline Site Scanner to projects that fight misinformation using AI. My goal is to keep growing in cybersecurity, technology leadership, and innovation, while sharing knowledge with my community.
